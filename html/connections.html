What does The Book of Mormon Teach?

The purpose of the Book of Mormon is to teach about Jesus Christ and His gospel. It is a book of scripture similar to the Bible. It was found and translated by Joseph Smith, who translated it through the power of God. The Book of Mormon contains the teachings of many prophets, helping us to better understand the doctrine of Christ. In this page, I have included the full video produced by The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints for each of my favorite lessons in the Book of Mormon. Below each video I have included a short summary of what the video contains.

Lehi teaches
This video shows Lehi's last interactions with his children and grandchildren before he dies. He teaches them the Plan of Salvation. This plan helps us understand why we are here on earth, what our purpose is, and what happens after we die. Knowing the answers to these questions helps put our life in perspective and help us feel more secure.

Jacob teaches
This video gives the warnings of the prophet Jacob, Nephi's younger brother. He teaches the people to beware of pride and to seek after God's Kingdom He also instructs the Nephite men to love their wives and children, and to completely abstain from adultery.

King Benajmin teaches
This video shows the teachings of the King Benjamin. He was a king that spent his time serving his people, and he instructs them to serve each other and live peaceably. He also teaches them about Jesus Christ, how he would come to the world as a man, but save us all from our sins by sacrificing himself for us.

Jesus teaches
This video contains the teachings of Jesus to the Nephite people after he died and was resurrected. He gives them the Sermon on the Mount, and tells them how to live His Law.
